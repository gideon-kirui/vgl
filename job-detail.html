Secretary

KFA, Nakuru Full Time

Job description

We are seeking a skilled and organized secretary to join our team. The ideal candidate will play a crucial role in maintaining efficient office operations and providing administrative support to various departments. The secretary will be responsible for handling communication, managing schedules, coordinating meetings, and performing general clerical tasks..

Responsibility

  • Answer and direct phone calls, take messages, and respond to inquiries.
  • Manage and organize office files, documents, and records.
  • Prepare and edit correspondence, reports, and presentations
  • Handle incoming and outgoing mail and emails.
  • Order and maintain office supplies.

Qualifications

  • Primary experience as a secretary or administrative assistant.
  • Proficient in MS Office (Word, Excel, Outlook) and other relevant software.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong organizational and multitasking abilities.
  • Attention to detail and accuracy
